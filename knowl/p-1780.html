Let \(A\) and \(B\) be invertible \(n \times n\) matrices. Then

  1. \(\left(A^{-1}\right)^{-1} = A\text{.}\)

  2. The product \(AB\) is invertible and \((AB)^{-1} = B^{-1}A^{-1}\text{.}\)

  3. The matrix \(A^{\tr}\) is invertible and \(\left(A^{\tr}\right)^{-1} = \left(A^{-1}\right)^{\tr}\text{.}\)
