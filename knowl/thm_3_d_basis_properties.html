Theorem 15.4.

Let \(W\) be a subspace of \(\R^n\) of dimension \(k\) and let \(S\) be a subset of \(W\) containing exactly \(k\) vectors.

  1. If \(S\) is linearly independent, then \(S\) is a basis for \(W\text{.}\)

  2. If \(S\) spans \(W\text{,}\) then \(S\) is a basis for \(W\text{.}\)
