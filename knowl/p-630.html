A linear combination of vectors \(\vv_1\text{,}\) \(\vv_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\vv_m\) in \(\R^n\) is any vector of the form

\begin{equation} c_1 \vv_1 + c_2 \vv_2 + \cdots + c_m \vv_m\text{,}\tag{4.1} \end{equation}

where \(c_1\text{,}\) \(c_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(c_m\) are scalars that we will refer to as the weights.
