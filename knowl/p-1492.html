Let \(A^{\tr} = [a'_{ij}]\text{.}\) Then \(a'_{ij} = a_{ji}\) by definition of the transpose. Let \(\left(A^{\tr}\right)^{\tr} = [a''_{ij}]\text{.}\) Then \(a''_{ij} = a'_{ji} = a_{ij}\text{.}\) So the \(ij\)th entry of \(\left(A^{\tr}\right)^{\tr}\) is the same as the \(ij\)th entry of \(A\text{,}\) and we conclude that \(\left(A^{\tr}\right)^{\tr} = A\text{.}\)
