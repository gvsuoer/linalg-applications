Carbon dioxide, \(\text{ CO } _2\text{,}\) is a familiar product of combustion. For example, when we burn glucose, \(\text{C} _6\text{H} _{12}\text{O} _6\text{,}\) the products of the reaction are carbon dioxide and water:

\begin{equation} \text{C} _6\text{H} _{12}\text{O} _6 + \text{O} _2 \to \text{ CO } _2 + \text{H} _2\text{O}\text{.}\tag{3.3} \end{equation}

Use the techniques developed in this project to balance this reaction.
