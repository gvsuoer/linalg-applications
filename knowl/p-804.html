This general system can also be written in the vector form

\begin{equation*} x_1 \left[ \begin{array}{c} a_{11} \\ a_{21} \\ \vdots \\ a_{m1} \end{array} \right] + x_2 \left[ \begin{array}{c} a_{12} \\ a_{22} \\ \vdots \\ a_{m2} \end{array} \right] + \cdots + x_n \left[ \begin{array}{c} a_{1n} \\ a_{2n} \\ \vdots \\ a_{mn} \end{array} \right] = \left[ \begin{array}{c} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_m \end{array} \right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
