\begin{equation*} p(\lambda) = (-1)^{n} (\lambda-\lambda_1)(\lambda- \lambda_2) \cdots (\lambda - \lambda_n)\text{.} \end{equation*}
(ii)

Explain why \(p(0) = \lambda_1 \lambda_2 \cdots \lambda_n\text{.}\)

(iii)
