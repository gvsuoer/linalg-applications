Focus Questions.

By the end of this section, you should be able to give precise and thorough answers to the questions listed below. You may want to keep these questions in mind to focus your thoughts as you complete the section

  • What is the dimension of a subspace of \(\R^n\text{?}\) What property of bases makes the dimension a well-defined number?

  • If \(W\) is a subspace of \(\R^n\) with dimension \(k\text{,}\) what must be true about any linearly independent subset \(S\) of \(W\) that contains exactly \(k\) vectors?

  • If \(W\) is a subspace of \(\R^n\) with dimension \(k\text{,}\) what must be true about any subset \(S\) of \(W\) that contains exactly \(k\) vectors and spans \(W\text{?}\)

  • What is the rank of a matrix?

  • What does the Rank-Nullity Theorem say?
