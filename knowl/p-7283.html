We can draw pictures of complex numbers in the plane. We let the \(x\)-axis be the real axis for a complex number and the \(y\)-axis the imaginary axis. That is, if \(z=a+bi\) we can think of \(z\) as a directed line segment from the origin to the point \((a,b)\text{,}\) where the terminal point of the segment is \(a\) units from the imaginary axis and \(b\) units from the real axis.
