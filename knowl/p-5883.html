The second eigenvector of \(XX^{\tr}\) also has meaning. A picture of the eigenspace \(E_{\lambda_2}\) corresponding to the smaller eigenvector \(\lambda_2\) of \(XX^{\tr}\) is shown in Figure 33.11.
