Task 22.8.a.ii.

\(\det(ABA^{\mathsf{T}})\)

Solution.

Assume that \(\det(A)=3\) and \(\det(B)=2\text{.}\)

We know that \(\det(A^{\tr}) = \det(A)\text{,}\) so

\begin{align*} \det(ABA^\tr) \amp = \det(A) \det(B) \det(A^{\tr})\\ \amp = \det(A) \det(B) \det(A)\\ \amp = (3)(2)(3)\\ \amp = 18\text{.} \end{align*}
