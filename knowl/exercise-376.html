Exercise 12.

Let \(\CB = \{[1 \ 1 \ 1]^{\tr}, [1 \ -1 \ 0]^{\tr}\}\) and let \(W = \Span \ \CB\) in \(\R^3\text{.}\)

(a)

Show that \(\CB\) is an orthogonal basis for \(W\text{,}\) using the dot product as inner product.

(b)

Explain why the vector \(\vv = [0 \ 2 \ 2]^{\tr}\) is not in \(W\text{.}\)

(c)

Find the vector in \(W\) that is closest to \(\vv\text{.}\) How close is this vector to \(\vv\text{?}\)
