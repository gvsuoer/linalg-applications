Exercise 7.

Let \(\CB = \{1, t, 1+t^2\}\) in \(\pol_2\text{.}\)

(a)

Show that \(\CB\) is a basis for \(\pol_2\text{.}\)

(b)

Let \(p_1(t) = 1+2t^2\text{,}\) \(p_2(t)=1+t+2t^2\text{,}\) and \(p_3(t) = 2-t+t^2\) in \(\pol_2\text{.}\)

(i)

Find \([p_1(t)]_{\CB}\text{,}\) \([p_2(t)]_{\CB}\text{,}\) and \([p_3(t)]_{\CB}\text{.}\)

(ii)

Use the coordinate vectors in part i. to determine if the set \(\{p_1(t), p_2(t), p_3(t)\}\) is linearly independent or dependent.
