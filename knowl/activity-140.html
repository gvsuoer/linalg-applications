Activity 35.8.

Consider \(\pol_2\) with the inner product \(\langle p(t), q(t) \rangle = \int_0^1 p(t)q(t) \ dt\text{.}\)

(a)

Find \(\langle p(t), 1-t \rangle\) where \(p(t)=a+bt+ct^2\) is in \(\pol_2\text{.}\)

(b)

Describe as best you can the orthogonal complement of \(\Span\{1-t\}\) in \(\pol_2\text{.}\) Is \(p(t)=1-2t-2t^2\) in this orthogonal complement? Is \(p(t)=1+t-t^2\text{?}\)
