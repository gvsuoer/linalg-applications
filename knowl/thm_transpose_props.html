Theorem 8.7.

Let \(A\) and \(B\) be matrices of the appropriate sizes and let \(a\) be a scalar. Then

  1. \(\displaystyle \left(A^{\tr}\right)^{\tr} = A\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (A+B)^{\tr} = A^{\tr} + B^{\tr}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (AB)^{\tr} = B^{\tr}A^{\tr}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle (aA)^{\tr} = aA^{\tr}\)
