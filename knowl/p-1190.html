In other words, there may be vectors \(\vb\) in the codomain of \(T\) that are not the image of any vector in the domain of \(T\text{.}\) If it is the case for a matrix transformation \(T\) that there is always a vector \(\vx\) in the domain of \(T\) such that \(T(\vx) = \vb\) for any vector \(\vb\) in the codomain of \(T\text{,}\) then \(T\) is given a special name.
