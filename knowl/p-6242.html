The Frobenius inner product is defined as

\begin{equation*} \langle A, B \rangle = \trace\left(AB^{\tr}\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}

for \(n \times n\) matrices \(A\) and \(B\text{.}\) Verify that \(\langle A, B \rangle\) defines an inner product on \(\M_{n \times n}\text{.}\)
