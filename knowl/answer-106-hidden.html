If \(A=\left[ \begin{array}{cr} \cos(\alpha) \amp -\sin(\alpha) \\ \sin(\alpha) \amp \cos(\alpha) \end{array} \right]\text{,}\) \(B = \left[ \begin{array}{cr} \cos(\beta) \amp -\sin(\beta) \\ \sin(\beta) \amp \cos(\beta) \end{array} \right]\text{,}\) then \(AB\) equals \(\left[ \begin{array}{cr} \cos(\alpha+\beta) \amp -\sin(\alpha+\beta) \\ \sin(\alpha+\beta) \amp \cos(\alpha+\beta) \end{array} \right]\)
