This means that \(A \vx = \vzero\) has a nontrivial solution. It follows that the vector equation (15.6) has a nontrivial solution and so the \(m\) vectors \(\vu_1\text{,}\) \(\vu_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\vu_m\) are linearly dependent.
