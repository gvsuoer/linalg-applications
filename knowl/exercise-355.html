Exercise 8.

Let \(W = \Span\{2+4t+6t^3, 3-t^2, 3-t^2+9t^3\}\) in \(\pol_3\text{.}\) Let \(\CB = \{1, t, t^2, t^3\}\) be the standard basis for \(\pol_3\text{.}\)

(a)

Calculate \([2+4t+6t^3]_{\CB}\text{,}\) \([3-t^2]_{\CB}\) and \([3-t^2+9t^3]_{\CB}\text{.}\)

(b)

Use the coordinate vectors from part (a) to determine if the polynomials \(2+4t+6t^3\text{,}\) \(3-t^2\text{,}\) and \(3-t^2+9t^3\) are linearly independent or dependent.

(c)

Let \(p(t) = 4+2t-t^2+9t^3\text{.}\) Find \([p(t)]_{\CB}\text{.}\)

(d)

Use the calculations from parts (a) and (c) to determine if \(p(t)\) is in \(W\text{.}\) If so, write \(p(t)\) as a linear combination of the polynomials \(2+4t+6t^3\text{,}\) \(3-t^2\text{,}\) and \(3-t^2+9t^3\text{.}\) If not, explain why not.
