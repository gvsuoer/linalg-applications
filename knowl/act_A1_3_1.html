Activity 4.2.

We work with vectors in \(\R^2\) to make the notation easier.

Let \(a\) be an arbitrary scalar, and \(\vu = \left[ \begin{array}{c} u_1\\ u_2 \end{array} \right]\) and \(\vv = \left[ \begin{array}{c} v_1\\ v_2 \end{array} \right]\) be two arbitrary vectors in \(\R^2\text{.}\) Is \(a(\vu + \vv)\) equal to \(a\vu + a\vv\text{?}\) What property does this imply about the scalar multiplication and addition operations on vectors?
