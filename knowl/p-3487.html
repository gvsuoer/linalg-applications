If \(\lambda_1\) is a strictly dominant eigenvalue, then \(\vx_k\) is approximately a scalar multiple of \(\vv_1\) for large values of \(k\text{,}\) regardless of the initial state \(\vx_0\text{.}\) In other words, large state vectors are close to eigenvectors for \(\lambda_1\text{.}\)
