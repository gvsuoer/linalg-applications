Let \(\vu\) and \(\vv\) be orthogonal vectors in an inner product space \(V\text{.}\) Then

\begin{align*} || \vu - \vv ||^2 \amp = \langle \vu-\vv, \vu-\vv \rangle\\ \amp = \langle \vu,\vu \rangle - 2 \langle \vu,\vv \rangle + \langle \vv, \vv \rangle\\ \amp = \langle \vu,\vu \rangle - 2 (0) + \langle \vv, \vv \rangle\\ \amp = ||\vu||^2 + ||\vv||^2\text{.} \end{align*}
