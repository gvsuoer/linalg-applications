Project Activity 34.6.

(a)

Let \(k\) be a positive integer. Explain why \(p_k(0) = p_k(1) = p_k(2) = \cdots = p_k(k-1) = 0\) and \(p_k(k) = 1\text{.}\)

(b)

Let \(\CP_n = \{p_0(t), p_1(t), p_2(t), \ldots, p_n(t)\}\text{.}\) Show that \(\CP_n\) is a basis for \(\pol_n\text{.}\) (Hint: Let \(c_1\text{,}\) \(c_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(c_n\) be scalars and consider the equation

\begin{equation*} c_0p_0(t) + c_1p_1(t) + c_2 p_2(t) + \cdots + c_np_n(t) = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}

Evaluate this equation at \(t=0\text{,}\) \(t=1\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(t=n\) and use the result of part (a).)
