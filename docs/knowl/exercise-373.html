Exercise 9.

Let \(V\) be an inner product space and \(\vu, \vv\) be two vectors in \(V\text{.}\)

(a)

Check that if \(\vv=\vzero\text{,}\) the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality

\begin{equation*} |\langle \vu, \vv \rangle | \leq ||\vu || ||\vv || \end{equation*}

holds.

Hint.
(b)

Assume \(\vv \neq \vzero\text{.}\) Let \(\lambda= \langle \vu, \vv \rangle / ||\vv||^2\) and \(\vw=\vu - \lambda \vv\text{.}\) Use the fact that \(||\vw||^2 \geq 0\) to conclude the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality in this case.
