The matrix on the left is the matrix of the coefficients of the system, and is called the coefficient matrix of the system. The matrix on the right is the matrix of coefficients and the constants, and is called the augmented matrix of the system (where we say we augment the coefficient matrix with the additional column of constants). We will separate the augmented column from the coefficient matrix with a vertical line to keep it clear that the last column is an augmented column of constants and not a column of coefficients. 4
