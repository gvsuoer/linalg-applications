Apply the power method to \(B = (A - 0 I_2)^{-1} = A^{-1}\text{.}\) As \(k\) increases, the vectors \(B^k \vx_0\) are approaching the vector \(\frac{1}{2}[1 \ -1]^{\tr}\text{,}\) which is an eigenvector of \(A\text{.}\) The Rayleigh quotients approach the other eigenvalue \(-1\) of \(A\text{.}\)
