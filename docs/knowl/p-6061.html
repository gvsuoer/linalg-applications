This mapping is an inner product on the space \(\M_{n \times n}\) called the Frobenius inner product (details are in the exercises). Let \(A = \left[ \begin{array}{cc} 1\amp 0\\2\amp 2 \end{array} \right]\) and \(B = \left[ \begin{array}{rc} -3\amp 4\\-2\amp 1 \end{array} \right]\) in \(\M_{2 \times 2}\text{.}\)
