(a)

Describe geometrically as best as you can the subspaces of \(\R^3\) spanned by the following sets of vectors. \(\left\{\left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\0 \end{array} \right]\right\}\) \(\left\{\left[ \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0\\0 \end{array} \right], \left[ \begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 1\\0 \end{array} \right]\right\}\)
