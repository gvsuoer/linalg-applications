Let \(\lambda_1\text{,}\) \(\lambda_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\lambda_k\) be \(k\) distinct eigenvalues for a matrix \(A\) and for each \(i\) between 1 and \(k\) let \(\vv_i\) be an eigenvector of \(A\) with eigenvalue \(\lambda_i\text{.}\) Then the vectors \(\vv_1\text{,}\) \(\vv_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(\vv_k\) are linearly independent.
