Answer 11.1.

The general solution to the system is

\begin{equation*} \left[ \begin{array}{c} x\\y\\z \end{array} \right] = y \left[ \begin{array}{c} 2\\1\\0 \end{array} \right] + z \left[ \begin{array}{r} -1\\0\\1 \end{array} \right] + \left[ \begin{array}{c} 3\\0\\0 \end{array} \right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

The solution is the plane in \(\R^3\) through the points \((3,0,0)\text{,}\) \((5,1,0)\text{,}\) and \((2,0,1)\text{.}\)
