If \(\CB = \{\vv_1, \vv_2, \ldots, \vv_m\}\) is an orthonormal basis for a subspace \(W\) of an inner product space and \(\vx\) is a vector in \(W\text{,}\) then (35.1) becomes

\begin{equation} \vx = \langle \vx, \vv_1 \rangle \vv_1 + \langle \vx, \vv_2 \rangle \vv_2 + \cdots + \langle \vx, \vv_m \rangle \vv_m\text{.}\tag{35.2} \end{equation}
