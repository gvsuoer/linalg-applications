Item.

If \(\CB = \{\vw_1, \vw_2, \ldots, \vw_m\}\) is an orthogonal basis for \(V\) and \(\vx \in V\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} [\vx]_{\CB} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} \frac{\langle \vx, \vw_1 \rangle}{\langle \vw_1, \vw_1 \rangle} \\ \frac{\langle \vx, \vw_2 \rangle}{\langle \vw_2, \vw_2 \rangle} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\langle \vx, \vw_m \rangle}{\langle \vw_m, \vw_m \rangle} \end{array} \right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
