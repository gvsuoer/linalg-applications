Let \(T(\vx) = A\vx\) with \(A \vv_1 = \lambda \vv_1\) and \(A \vv_2 = \lambda \vv_2 + \vv_1\text{.}\) Then \(T\) maps \(\Span \{\vv_1\}\) to \(\Span \{\vv_1\}\) by a factor of \(\lambda\text{,}\) and \(T\) maps \(\Span\{\vv_2\}\) to the line containing the terminal point of \(\vv_1\) in the direction of \(\vv_2\text{.}\) The matrix \(C\) performs a change of basis from the standard basis to the basis \(\{\vv_1, \vv_2\}\text{,}\) then the matrix \(\left[ \begin{array}{cc} 2\amp 1\\0\amp 2 \end{array} \right]\) performs an expansion by a factor of 2 in all directions and a shear in the direction of \(\vv_1\text{.}\)
