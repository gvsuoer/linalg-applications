A linear system

\begin{alignat*}{5} {a_{11}}x_1 \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{12}}x_2 \amp {}+{} \amp \cdots \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{1n}}x_n \amp {}={} \amp b_1\\ {a_{21}}x_1 \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{22}}x_2 \amp {}+{} \amp \cdots \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{2n}}x_n \amp {}={} \amp b_2\\ {} \amp {} \amp {} \amp {} \amp \vdots \ \ \amp {} \amp {} \amp {} \amp {}\\ {a_{m1}}x_1 \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{2m}}x_2 \amp {}+{} \amp \cdots \amp {}+{} \amp {a_{mn}}x_n \amp {}={} \amp b_m \end{alignat*}

can be written in matrix form as

\begin{equation*} A \vx = \vb\text{,} \end{equation*}

where

\begin{equation*} A = \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} a_{11} \amp a_{12} \amp \cdots \amp a_{1n} \\ a_{21} \amp a_{22} \amp \cdots \amp a_{2n} \\ \vdots \amp \amp \ddots \amp \vdots \\ a_{m1} \amp a_{m2} \amp \cdots \amp a_{mn} \end{array} \right], \ \ \ \vx = \left[ \begin{array}{c} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ \vdots \\ x_n \end{array} \right], \ \ \ \text{ and } \ \ \ \vb = \left[ \begin{array}{c} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_m \end{array} \right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
