Let \(P\) be an \(n \times n\) orthogonal matrix and let \(\vx, \vy \in \R^n\text{.}\) Then

  1. \((P\vx) \cdot (P\vy) = \vx \cdot \vy\text{,}\)

  2. \(||P\vx|| = ||\vx||\text{,}\) and

  3. \(\frac{P\vx \cdot P\vy}{||P\vx|| \ ||P\vy||} = \frac{\vx \cdot \vy}{||\vx|| \ ||\vy||}\) if \(\vx\) and \(\vy\) are nonzero.
