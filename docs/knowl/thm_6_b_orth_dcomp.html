Theorem 24.4.

Let \(\CB = \{\vv_1, \vv_2, \ldots, \vv_m\}\) be an orthogonal basis for a subspace of \(\R^n\text{.}\) Let \(\vx\) be a vector in \(W\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation} \vx = \ds \frac{\vx \cdot \vv_1}{\vv_1 \cdot \vv_1} \vv_1 + \frac{\vx \cdot \vv_2}{\vv_2 \cdot \vv_2} \vv_2 + \cdots + \frac{\vx \cdot \vv_m}{\vv_m \cdot \vv_m} \vv_m\text{.}\tag{24.3} \end{equation}
