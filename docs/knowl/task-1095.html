(i)

Show that \(\lambda^2 + \lambda - 4\) is the characteristic polynomial for \(B\text{.}\)

(ii)

Calculate \(B^2\text{.}\) Then compute \(B^2 + B - 4I_2\text{.}\) What do you get?
